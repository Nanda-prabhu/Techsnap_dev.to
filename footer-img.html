Communities building on Forem

DEV was created in 2016 to be a place for developers from all over the world to gather and share their knowledge. Today, DEV is a passionate community of over 800k+ members and 10+ million hits per month. DEV is where developers, tech professionals, and thought leaders go to connect, learn, and freely editorialize.

One of the web's foremost thought leaders in the community space is Community Club, a space for community builders by community builders. Community Club chose Forem for its commitment to open source, inclusive ethos, safety tooling, and content syndication capabilities.
‍

Web Monetization leverages Forem for their community dedicated to improving business models on the web for creators, publishers, and consumers alike. Their efforts are backed by Grant for the Web and are dedicated to boosting open, fair, and inclusive standards and innovation through open protocols.
